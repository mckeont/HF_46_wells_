These are the 46 HF wells selected by Krissy.

The orange circles are 1000 foot buffers from each well.

The light blue polylines within the buffers are segments of streams/rivers within 1000 feet of a HF well.

The darker blue polylines outside of the buffers are major rivers in PA. Hover over each well and stream/river to learn more info.
